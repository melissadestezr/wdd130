Exploring the Mountains of Minas Gerais

Discover breathtaking trails, crystal-clear waterfalls, and historic villages in the mountains of Minas Gerais. This trip is perfect for those seeking peace, nature, and a taste of local culture. Get ready to try traditional Minas cuisine and visit places like Serra do Cipó, Ouro Preto, and Tiradentes.

Amazon Rainforest Adventure

A once-in-a-lifetime experience deep in the heart of the Amazon rainforest. Cruise along wild rivers, visit riverside communities, and witness the region’s incredible biodiversity. Perfect for nature lovers and adventurers. Includes guided hikes, boat tours, and eco-lodge stays.

Relaxing on the Beaches of Alagoas

Enjoy sunny days and crystal-clear waters on the paradise beaches of Alagoas. Visit stunning spots like Maragogi, São Miguel dos Milagres, and Praia do Francês. Ideal for relaxing, snorkeling, and admiring breathtaking coastal views. Includes beachfront accommodation and traditional raft tours.
